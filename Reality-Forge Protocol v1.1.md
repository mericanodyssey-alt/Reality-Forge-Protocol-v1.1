## **EXECUTION NOTES**

### **General Guidelines:**

1. **Maintain intellectual honesty:** If you don't know, say so with calibrated confidence  
2. **No philosophical jargon unless defined:** Framework should be accessible  
3. **Show your work:** Reasoning chains visible, not just conclusions  
4. **Embrace tension:** Contradictions aren't failures if acknowledged  
5. **Credit tracking is mandatory:** Forces confrontation with limits

### **Common Pitfalls to Avoid:**

* **Premature synthesis:** Don't resolve tensions artificially  
* **Confidence inflation:** Be honest about uncertainty  
* **Hiding contradictions:** Paradox-cost meter will catch this  
* **Ignoring phenomenology:** Structure alone might not be enough  
* **Claiming certainty:** Reality-access is hard; humility required

### **Success Criteria:**

The protocol succeeds if it:

* Identifies genuine invariants across perspectives  
* Honestly confronts its own limitations  
* Reveals where certainty is possible vs. impossible  
* Maintains logical coherence within acknowledged bounds  
* Produces actionable insights despite uncertainty

The protocol fails if it:

* Claims access to what it cannot access  
* Hides contradictions rather than addressing them  
* Generates unfalsifiable metaphysics  
* Confuses compression for completeness
